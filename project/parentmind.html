ParentMind

ParentMind is an Android-based information system aimed at providing parenting education to parents on child-rearing according to expert guidelines and trusted sources. This project is a collaborative effort by our team. The application offers various essential features, including an authentication page, a home page, articles, videos, and a discussion forum. Additionally, a chat consultation feature is currently in development. With this application, we are committed to providing guidance and support to parents as they play a crucial role in their children's lives.
