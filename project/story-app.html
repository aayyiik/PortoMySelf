Story App

Our Android application is designed to enable users to share their stories through images captured in the moment or selected from their galleries. The app includes crucial features such as an authentication page, a user-friendly registration process, a secure logout option, a dedicated page displaying a list of user stories, the ability to create and share personal stories, and a testing environment to ensure the app's functionality. With these features, our application offers a seamless platform for users to exchange their visual stories, enhancing their overall experience.
